About us

At Build Rite, we specialize in constructing top-quality fences using the finest handpicked materials, ensuring durability and beauty for your property.

Our pricing starts at $36 per linear foot for a new 6-foot-tall fence, featuring pressure-treated posts, redwood rails, and 1x4x6 redwood fence boards with a dog-eared top. This price also includes demolition and removal of your old fence. For those interested in metal posts, we offer "Postmaster" brand metal posts for an additional $3 per linear foot.
